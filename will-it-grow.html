Will moss grow in my spot?

Moss lives or dies by a few simple conditions. Answer these four questions about the place you have in mind and you will get an honest sense of how it is likely to do, and which sort of moss to reach for. Nothing is sent anywhere; it all works in your browser.

    Whatever the verdict, the method is in the growing guide. For a lawn see how to make a moss lawn, and for the cushion-versus-carpet question, acrocarpous and pleurocarpous mosses.
